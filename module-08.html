8.9 Procedures

8.9.1 Lab Safety

8.9.1.1 Before Lab

  • Wear lab coat, goggles, and gloves.
  • Wipe the bench and pipettors with 70% ethanol.

8.9.1.2 During Lab

  • Treat all tips as potential biohazards.
  • Remove PPE when leaving the lab.
  • Discard samples and disposables correctly.
  • Minimize use of personal devices.

8.9.1.3 After Lab

  • Wipe the station and any touched personal items with 70% ethanol.
  • Dispose of gloves, plates, liquids, and glass in the correct locations.
  • Return reusable materials, remove PPE, and wash your hands.
